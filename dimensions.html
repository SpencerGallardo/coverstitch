Game & Movie Case Dimensions

Exact cover dimensions for every major case format — in millimeters, pixels at 300 DPI, and spine widths. Use these when choosing a preset in CoverStitch.

All pixel values are calculated at 300 DPI — the standard for high-quality print output. CoverStitch uses these exact dimensions in its built-in presets, so your output will fit any of these cases without trimming.

How to use this guide Match your physical case to a format below, then select that preset in CoverStitch before stitching. The tool will resize your front, back, and spine images to the exact pixel dimensions shown — ready to print at 100% scale with no resizing in your print dialog.

About these measurements Dimensions are based on standard retail case sizes measured from physical cases. Minor variations exist between manufacturers and regions — if a cover comes out slightly off, measure your specific case and use the Custom preset in CoverStitch with your exact dimensions.
